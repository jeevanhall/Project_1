Strong Coffee, Strong Bonds Meet Our Farmers

We partner with farmers from all over the globe to provide the best quality coffee while helping our farm partners mantain a sustainiable and profitable platform. Our farmers grow different types of coffee ranging from Arabica to Robusta. In the next section we will hilight the locale, altitude, coffee bean characteristics, body and other details about 4 farms which we source our coffee from. Two are from Africa and the other two are from India.

We guarantee that you will fall in love with our simple, yet profound coffee the moment you take your first sip. Whether you are on a daily routine or entertaining company you will be blown away by the purity and quality of our local gobally sourced coffee.

Buku Guji-Hambela

Country: Ethiopia
Region: Guji-Hambela
Contact: Animaw Ansari
Varietal: Heirloom
Process: Natural
Species: Arabica
Altitude: 2,200 - 2,300 MASL
Notes: Concord Grape, Brownie, Coconut, Pear
Recommended Roast:City to Full City
Good For:Pour Over, Auto Drip, Espresso

Kisinga Washing Station RWENZORI-Bukonzo

Country: Uganda
Region: Rwenzori-Kisinga
Contact: Ruben Kane
Varietal: SL14, SL28
Process: Natural
Species: Arabica
Altitude: 2,100 MASL
Notes:heavy body, jammy acidity and crisp notes of blackberry with a round, chocolaty finish
Recommended Roast:Bold
Good For:Pour Over, Auto Drip, French Press

Gungeri Estate Chikmagalur

Country: India
Region: Karnataka-Chikmagalur
Contact: Zecharias Das
Varietal:Peridenia
Process: Natural
Species: Robusta
Altitude: 950 MASL
Notes:sweet medium body, light acidity
Recommended Roast:Bold
Good For:Espresso, Pour Over

Anantigiri Araku Valley

Country: India
Region: Andra Pradesh-Araku Valey
Contact: Soona Gupta
Varietal: S.795, Sln.6, & Sln.9
Process: Natural
Species: Robusta
